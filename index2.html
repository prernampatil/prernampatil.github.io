Prerna M Patil

I am a postdoctoral researcher at University of Washington's AI Institute in Dynamic Systems working with Prof. Steven Brunton and Prof. Nathan Kutz since September 2023.

Prior to this, I completed my doctorate in the Computational Modeling and Simulation (CMS) program at the University of Pittsburgh, under the mentorship of Prof. Hessam Babaee. I received my Master's (specializing in Fluids and Thermal Sciences) from Brown University, RI. You can read my Ph.D. dissertation, titled "Real-time reduced order modeling of high-dimensional partial differential equations via time dependent subspaces"
